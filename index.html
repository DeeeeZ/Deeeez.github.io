§ IV Table of Contents

How we
do this.

4 – 8 weeks · four acts

I don't consult. I code. I don't sell licenses — I solve problems. The tool (Python, RPA, Power Platform) is chosen for what works, not what generates the highest license fee.

Walk through your finance team's monthly workflow. Find where time disappears. Model the ROI. You'll get a clear number: "This automation will save 400 hours/month" — or "Not worth it, here's why." I tell you the truth, not the pitch.

You approve the blueprint. I show which systems talk to which, what data gets pulled, where errors are caught, how reports generate. You sign off before any code starts. No surprises at go-live.

I build it. Your team tests it with real data. I fix it. We don't deploy until the actual users have sat with it for a full week and said, "This is going to change how we work."

Your team goes live. I stay for 30 days — monitoring, debugging edge cases, training. After 30 days we measure: did we hit that 400 hours/month? (We always do.)
